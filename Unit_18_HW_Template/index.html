Anthony Rodriguez

Hello! I am, Anthony Rodriguez. I’m a passionate UI/UX designer who thrives on using creativity to craft intuitive and visually engaging digital experiences. My work involves blending artistic vision with user-centered design principles to transform complex challenges into simple, elegant solutions. With a strong focus on creating user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing interfaces, I use my problem-solving skills to deliver designs that are not only user-friendly but also visually pleasing. When I’m not immersed in design, you’ll often find me exploring new places. Traveling fuels my creativity and broadens my perspective, reinforcing my belief that continuous learning is critical for success. The ever-evolving world of design offers endless opportunities for growth, and I’m always motivated to refine my skills and embrace new trends.

My UX Skills

User Research

For user research, I typically will conduct interviews, create a user persona, and create empathy maps to fully understand where the business currently stands. This aids me in the ability to fully comprehend what we need to do in order to design according to the needs of the company and the user.

Wireframing

When it comes to wireframing, it is easier for me to put my thoughts down on paper. This allows me to place content where the research initially led me. From here I am able to see if there was something missed or if something needs to be redefined. It helps communicate the ideas as we begin to develop the work.

High Fidelity Prototyping

LPrototyping has become one of my joys. Because here, I am able to see my designs come to life. Understand what works and doesn’t work. Conduct user tests to further determine whether the design works or doesn’t. It also helps me see what improvements we are able to make.
